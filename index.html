Philippine Red Cross - Mobile Data Collection
The Philippine Red Cross' vision is to be the foremost humanitarian organization in the country. Embracing new and emerging technologies such as mobile data collection helps us achieve our mission to bring timely, effective and compassionate humanitarian assistance to the most vulnerable without consideration of nationality, race, creed, gender, social status or political belief.
This site has been created to provide training materials and other resources. It is under construction. For specific requests and questions please email daniel.joseph@ifrc.org
